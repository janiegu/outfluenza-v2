Over the past few decades, the world has witnessed a revolution in information technology. With developments such as personal computers, smartphones, and tablets, technology is being used to obtain, disseminate, and share information quicker and better than ever before. Moreover, advances in data analytics have given us powerful tools to organize the information available to us and draw meaningful conclusions. The Outfluenza team seeks to apply these technology and data solutions to building a powerful analytical engine that tracks the spread of influenza in real-time, provides beautiful visualizations to represent the data, and predicts outbreaks before they occur.
